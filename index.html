About Us

TRRRR LLC is a physician-founded medical device startup dedicated to transforming central vascular access. We've developed a groundbreaking single-handed device that enables venipuncture and guidewire passage under continuous ultrasound guidance, eliminating blind guidewire advancement and significantly reducing complication risks associated with the traditional Seldinger technique

We are currently in our pre-seed phase, raising capital to advance our product to commercial-grade, secure FDA Class II 510(k) clearance, and launch with early clinical adopters.

The VascuWire Device

  • Combining Needle Aspiration, Stabilization, and Wire Passage
  • Enabling Continuous Ultrasound Imaging
  • Offering One-Handed Control
  • Enhancing Safety
  • Improving Efficiency

Initial physician testing and a mannequin model study have demonstrated strong interest and ease of uptake, highlighting the significant clinical value of our solution.
